B.2.4 Minimum Range Length

38.1513GPPNRRelease 17TSUser Equipment (UE) Multiple Input Multiple Output (MIMO) Over-the-Air (OTA) performance requirements

Tools: ARFCN - Frequency Conversion for 5G NR/LTE/UMTS/GSM

The minimum range length of FR2 MPAC system is defined as the distance from the centre of the test zone to the aperture of the measurement probes/antennas, as illustrated in Figure B.2.4-1.

Figure B.2.4-1: Illustration of range length definition of FR2 3D-MPAC

The minimum range length for NR FR2 MPAC OTA systems with 20cm test zone size is 0.75m. It was shown that the PSP can be reduced significantly for distances below 0.75m.
