5G NR/LTE ARFCN-Frequency Caculator (3GPP Specs)

Formulas

5G NR-ARFCN Frequency calculation (38.104)

FREF = FREF-Offs + ΔFGlobal (NREF – NREF-Offs)

LTE E-ARFCN Frequency calculation (36.101)

FDL = FDL_low + 0.1(NDL – NOffs-DL)

FUL = FUL_low + 0.1(NUL – NOffs-UL)

UMTS U-ARFCN Frequency calculation (25.101, 25.102)

FDL = FDL_Offset + 0.2(NDL)

FDL = FUL_Offset + 0.2(NUL)

GSM ARFCN Frequency calculation (45.001)

FDL = FDL_Offset + 0.2(NDL – NOffs-DL)

FUL = FUL_Offset + 0.2(NUL– NOffs-UL)
